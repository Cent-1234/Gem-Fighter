FAQs

What are Gem Fighter NFTs?

Gem Fighter NFTs are unique, animated voxel gems that upgrade your spaceship's abilities and unlock rewards within the Gem Fighter game ecosystem.

What’s the mint price?

The mint price is currently fixed at $36 worth of BNB per NFT. The exact BNB amount is auto-converted based on current market rates.

How can I stake them?

You can stake your Gem Fighter NFTs on the staking platform to earn $HODL tokens as rewards. This increases your earnings passively while holding the NFT.

What wallet do I need?

You need a web3-compatible wallet such as MetaMask or WalletConnect to connect and mint your NFTs.

Are there any limits?

Yes, there is a max mint quantity per transaction to ensure fair distribution. The total supply is capped at 50,000 NFTs.
